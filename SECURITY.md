# Security Policy for TypedConfig (Tyco)

If you discover a security vulnerability in Tyco or any of its language bindings, please report it responsibly.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

- Email us at info@typedconfig.com with details of the vulnerability.
- Do not open a public issue for security-related matters.
- We will respond as quickly as possible and coordinate a fix.

## Supported Versions

We support the latest major and minor releases of Tyco and its official language bindings.

Thank you for helping keep TypedConfig safe!
